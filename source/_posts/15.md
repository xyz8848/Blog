1. 打开 [https://aad.portal.azure.com/#view/Microsoft_AAD_IAM/TenantPropertiesBlade](https://aad.portal.azure.com/#view/Microsoft_AAD_IAM/TenantPropertiesBlade)
2. 点击下方 “管理安全默认值”
3. 将 “安全默认值” 设置为 “已禁用”
4. 点击 “保存”
