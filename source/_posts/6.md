## 教程开始
### 安装“Nginx”
![](http://cdn.xyz8848.com/img/blog/6/1.png)  
![](http://cdn.xyz8848.com/img/blog/6/2.png)

### 配置“Nginx”
![](http://cdn.xyz8848.com/img/blog/6/3.png)
```
stream{
    server{
        listen 25565;
        proxy_pass mc.hypixel.net:25565;
    }
}
```
